Mission

Our goal is to provide current and former Columbia students with a virtual campus to reunite with classmates and professors. By working together as a community, users can help rebuild Columbia in all of its glory, block by block.

Features:
There is a Ranking System for different users (students, builders, alumni, passerby ) which you can read about in our FAQs!

All students will be able to build in Student Project areas.

Our server supports various Plugins, including World Edit for experienced Builder. More info will be release about the Builder rank soon!

This website will be replaced with a Fully Functional, Automatic site that allows students to confirm their association with Columbia and get started playing even faster!

How to be part of the community

As long as you are part of the columbia community (or an incoming student!), you are welcome!

1 - Join the Slack channel at columbiavirtualcampus.slack.com

2 - Send a message to #newuser channel with your columbia email! (or email elt2141@columbia.edu if you don't have Slack)

3 - Receive a reply message with the server's ip on your email! (have fun)
Note: For now we're only letting Columbia students join as our server currently only has the resources to support ~150 players. If we grow in popularity we will expand this number :)

4 - Upon joining the server you'll be granted the rank of Student can participate in Student Projects

Project inspiration

Everything started at Columbia University VIRTUAL CAMPUS Design Challenge

Students have expressed how disconnected they feel from campus as seen in multiple posts on Facebook including memes about people not knowing how to study outside of Butler. They expressed wanting “comfort and motivation for those of us missing Columbia” even as we are separated by time zones and different life situations amidst COVID-19.

In this way we are providing an opportunity for students to connect with each other “on campus” to combat these issues of loneliness and disconnectedness before the Fall through a fun interactive campus on Minecraft.
